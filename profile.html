About Cloud Sync

End-to-End Encryption

All your data is encrypted on your device before being sent to the cloud, using AES-256 encryption. This means only you can access your data, even we can't read it.

Automatic Syncing

Changes you make are automatically synced across all your devices. If you're offline, changes will be saved locally and synced when you're back online.

Privacy First

We never see your unencrypted data. Your encryption keys are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Multiple Devices

Use SlimEasy on your phone, tablet, and computer with the same account. All your data will be available on each device.
