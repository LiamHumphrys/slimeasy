Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2025

Summary

  • Local storage only: All your data stays on your device
  • No servers: We don't collect or transmit your data
  • No cookies: We don't use tracking cookies
  • Your control: You can export or delete your data anytime

Data Collection and Storage

SlimEasy is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here's how we handle your data:

What information we store

SlimEasy collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Account details (name and email)
  • Profile information (age, weight, height, gender, activity level, and goals)
  • Food and meal tracking data
  • Exercise records
  • Water intake tracking
  • App settings and preferences

How we store your data

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We don't use any external servers to process or store your information
  • Your data is only accessible from the browser and device where you entered it
  • Clearing your browser data will erase all stored information

GDPR Compliance

Even though SlimEasy doesn't collect or process your data on our servers, we respect and comply with GDPR principles:

Your Rights

  • Right to access: You can view all your data directly in the app
  • Right to data portability: You can export your data using the export functions
  • Right to be forgotten: You can delete your account and all associated data from the settings page
  • Data minimization: We only collect information necessary for the app's functionality

Notifications

SlimEasy may send notifications about:

  • Hydration reminders
  • Daily tracking reminders
  • Goal achievements

These notifications are processed entirely on your device and use the browser's notification API. You can disable notifications at any time through your browser settings or within the app.

Third-Party Services

SlimEasy uses the following third-party resources:

  • Font Awesome (for icons): Loaded from a CDN but doesn't transmit your personal data
  • Chart.js (for visualizations): Loaded from a CDN but doesn't transmit your personal data

These services provide visual elements and functionality but don't receive any of your personal information.

Data Deletion

You can remove all your data at any time by:

  1. Using the "Delete Account" function in the app
  2. Clearing your browser's localStorage manually
  3. Clearing browser data for this site
